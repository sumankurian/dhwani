Dhwani (Sound) that echo through the soul, awaken the mind and carry tradition to new horizons.

Chenda is a traditional South Indian percussion instrument, prominently used in the cultural and ritualistic music of Kerala, India.
Dhwani the Chenda Band in Halifax, Nova Scotia is creating a unique cultural bridge by bringing the vibrant rhythms of Kerala, India, to Canada. Founded by a group of passionate enthusiasts, this band brings the art of playing the chenda to the Canadian Community.

Highlights

Chendamelam

Dhwani offers vibrant Shinkarimelam performances, known for their fast-paced, high-energy rhythms that electrify any celebration. For those seeking a more traditional experience, we also present Pancharimelam, a harmonious blend of classic beats that embody the soul of Kerala’s cultural heritage

Chenda Fusion

Dhwani brings a modern twist to tradition with our Chenda-Violin Fusion, blending the soulful melodies of the violin with the dynamic beats of the chenda. For a high-energy contemporary vibe, our Chenda-DJ Fusion creates an electrifying synergy of traditional percussion and modern electronic music.

Other Indian Percussion

Dhwani extends its rhythmic expertise with performances featuring other Indian percussion instruments like the dhol, adding vibrant beats and dynamic energy to any event. These versatile offerings bring a rich, pan-Indian flavor, perfectly complementing our traditional chenda rhythms

Mathew Bichu Babu

Founder(Dhwani Atlantic)

Mathew - A talented musician has brought the rich tradition of Kerala’s Chendamelam to Atlantic Canada by training 30 individuals in the art of playing the Chenda, forming the region’s first-ever Chendamelam troupe. With dedication and passion, they introduced this vibrant percussion tradition to a diverse group, fostering cultural pride among the Indian community and sparking interest among Canadians. This initiative not only preserves a centuries-old art form but also builds a bridge between cultures, uniting people through the universal rhythm of music.

Welcome to Dhwani Atlantic - A Not for Profit Arts Club, where rhythm meets tradition!

Founded with a passion for preserving and promoting Kerala’s cultural heritage, Dhwani is a vibrant chendamelam troupe based in Atlantic Canada. We are a group of enthusiastic individuals united by our love for the pulsating rhythms of traditional Kerala percussion music and a shared mission to bring the soul of Kerala’s art forms to audiences far and wide.

Our journey began with the vision of creating a platform that celebrates the timeless traditions of chendamelam while fostering a sense of community and cultural pride among our members and audiences. Each performance is a blend of dedication, skill, and the joy of keeping our traditions alive.

Dhwani’s debut performance, part of the event Keraleeyam, marked a significant milestone in our journey. This program showcased not only our troupe’s talents but also the richness of Kerala’s diverse art forms, bringing a slice of Kerala’s vibrant culture to the Atlantic region.

At Dhwani, we believe that music transcends boundaries, and every beat of the chenda tells a story. Whether it’s a celebration, a cultural event, or a festival, we are committed to delivering performances that resonate with energy, tradition, and authenticity.

Join us as we continue this exciting journey, spreading the dynamic rhythms of Kerala’s heritage and creating unforgettable memories for audiences everywhere.
